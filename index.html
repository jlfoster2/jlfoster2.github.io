Hi there, I'm John Foster



John is a Partner and the Director of Programs for Fearless Solutions. He is responsible for the delivery of technical services and solutions to various customers within the Federal Government and the Intelligence Community. He personally engages on some projects as a hands-on leader and oversees software development, Agile training, coaching, and program management. With more than 15 years of experience working in software for both commercial and Government customers, he has become an advocate and enthusiast for Agile delivery and helping the Government to get stuff done! John is a certified Project Management Professional (PMP), a certified Scrum Master, obtained his B.S. in Computer Science from UMBC and his MBA from Loyola University Maryland.
